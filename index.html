Why this event?

Before my husband Brandon passed away, I promised to continue his dreams. One thing he really wanted was to make a difference in the world. To honor him and keep my promise, I organized Brandon's Community Walk, a 5K event all about raising awareness for mental health. Our aim with this event is to shine a light on mental health issues and support those who need it.
